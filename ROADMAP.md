Guiding Principles

1. Help people learn Portuguese through songs.
2. Portuguese is always shown first.
3. Translation follows immediately below each lyric.
4. One lyric pair is the basic learning unit.
5. Minimize manual formatting when importing songs.
6. Design for iPhone first.
7. AI should reduce work, not replace learning.
8. Listening and reading should happen together whenever possible.
